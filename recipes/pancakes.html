Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 Tbsp melted butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Add the milk, butter, and egg to the bowl, them mix together until smooth.
  3. Lightly oil a griddle and heat it over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  4. Cook the pancake until bubbles form and the edges dry, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Flip the pancake over and cook the other side until browned. Repeat steps 3-5 with remaining batter.
