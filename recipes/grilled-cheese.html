Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 8 slices cooked bacon
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices sourdough bread

Steps

  1. Mash butter and parmesan cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended.
  2. Make sandwiches by placing one slice of cheddar cheese and two bacon slices on half of the bread slices. Top with remaining bread.
  3. Heat a large skilled over medium heat. Spread some butter on the top of each sandwich, then place butter side down in the skillet.
  4. Spread butter on other side of sandwich, then cook each side until golden brown (about 3 minutes per side).
